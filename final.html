Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the KPR ERP Portal?

The KPR ERP Portal is a platform designed to streamline academic and administrative tasks for students and faculty.

2. How can I check my grades?

You can log in to the portal and navigate to the 'Academics' section to view your grades.

3. Who can access the portal?

Both students and faculty members can access the portal with their institutional login credentials.
